If you'd like to help or you have something to say about a result, e-mail map@solidaritypiedmont.org .

This map is a growing record of the businesses, nonprofits, and public places that provide ways to meet our needs without big corporations. More than a "buy local" movement, it identifies the practices existing in your community that are planting the seeds of a more sustainable people-and-planet economy.

Some suggested uses: use the map to try and meet all your needs with only used and free goods for a month, or get all of your food from gardens, CSAs, and co-ops. Next time you have a need, check here before going to a corporation and see how many of your needs can be met by your own neighbors.
